Options For Reporting

Some official reporting options are confidentially, anonymously, law enforcement, and Title IX.

Rights In School

Title IX is a federal law prohibiting discrimination on the basis of sex in education. Title IX addresses sexual violence and requires schools to proactively address, remedy, and eliminate sexual violence on their campuses.
