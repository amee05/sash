About SASH

SASH (Students Against Sexual Harassment) is a high school student-led club with an emphasis on the education of sexual harassment for youth. We believe that educating our fellow high schoolers about sexual harassment and its affects on society will help lessen the amount sexual harassment in the future. According to a survey by Stop Street Harassment in 2018, 81% of women and 43% of men experience some form of sexual harassment in their lifetime. This statistic is higher for people of marginalized communities. We hope to lower these statistics through educating students in high schools across the country.
